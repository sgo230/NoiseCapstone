In recent years, the growing volume of noise complaints in New York City has increased response times and hindered enforcement of the city’s noise code by the Department of Environmental Protection. In this Capstone project, we used supervised learning to assign complaint priority and produced a heatmap showing areas of complaint priority in order to optimize response to noise complaints.

Yes, New York is a noisy city.

The noise code was created to reduce the excessive and unreasonable noises within the city. The city's enforcement of the noise code is primarily complaint-based. Residents register a complaint via the 311 hotline and the complaint is directed to either the NYPD or NYC DEP.

  • NYC 311
  • NYC Noise Code


NYPD

The New York Polic Department (NYPD) handles noise complaints involving people to people interactions.

  • Clubs or Bars
  • Parks
  • Stores or businesses
  • Streets or sidewalks
  • Vehicles



NYC DEP

New York City Department of Environmental Protection (NYC DEP) handles complaints relating to equipment or repeating noise.

  • Construction
  • Pets and Animals
  • Food vending vehicles
  • Air conditioners
  • Motor vehicles

Prediction

Using supervised learning to predict complaints.

LSTM Neural Network

LSTM as an Undercomplete Autoencoder

An autoencoder neural network was used to model a spatiotemporal forecasting system for DEP noise complaint volume. The top image shows that the model succeeds in learning the periodicity of data, but fail to predict high-variational volume. In order to mitigate a severe penalization on a single random onset, as well as to reformulate the question such that the prediction can serve as an actionable insight to DEP, the Softmax function was added to the top of the network to convert predictions to pseudo probability. Using computed pseudo-probability as the rank of the neighborhood, the model’s performance was calculated as Precision at k accuracy, where the correctness of the membership in the top-ranked neighborhoods compared to the true membership computed from the ranking of the corresponding day.
